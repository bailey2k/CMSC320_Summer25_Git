
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Version control is incredibly important, especially if your drive fails.
This has happened to me personally and it was a nightmare that could have been
avoided with committing often.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Machine learning and artificial intelligence. Being able to learn from
massive quantities of data is a huge feature of AI models.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging allows for branching history while rebasing essentially just
adds on top of the existing main branch in a line. Rolling back with rebasing is more
difficult than merging commits.
